Common Inquiries

Everything you need to know about our artisanal services.

We currently offer premium temperature-controlled delivery across Lahore, Karachi, and Islamabad. Delivery zones include DHA, Bahria Town, and major metropolitan areas. For specific addresses, please use the locator at checkout.
For standard signature cakes, we require at least 48 hours notice. Custom artisanal cakes and wedding tiers require 7-10 business days for design consultation and ingredient sourcing to ensure perfection.
We accept all major credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard), Bank Transfers, and JazzCash. Cash on Delivery is available for orders under PKR 5,000 within specific city limits.
